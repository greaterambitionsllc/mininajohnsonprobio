About

She doesn't manage tasks.
She architects results.

Dr. Minina Johnson is not the average program manager. She is the one corporations and organizations call when execution has failed, cost is bleeding, and leadership needs someone who can walk in, take command, and deliver. With over 20 years of enterprise experience spanning defense contracting, federal government, aerospace, and professional services, Dr. Jaye has built a career at the intersection of strategy and execution.

She holds a Doctor of Business Administration (DBA) in Project Management from Capella University. Her landmark doctoral capstone — "Perceptions of African American Women Project Managers on Managing Projects and Career Progression in the Aerospace and Defense Industry" — was published in the Business Management Research and Applications: A Cross-Disciplinary Journal.

As Founder & Principal Strategist of Greater Ambitions LLC, a diversity-owned PM and Operations Consultancy based in Tucson, AZ, Dr. Jaye works directly with CEOs, COOs, and executive teams at Fortune 500 and Inc. 5000 organizations to close execution gaps and build operating infrastructure for sustained scale.

Organizational Leadership

Sigma Gamma Rho
Sorority, Inc.

Active Since 2016

Dr. Jaye's leadership within Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority, Inc. is not ceremonial. It is the same caliber of organizational transformation she delivers in the boardroom, applied to the sisterhood she has served for nearly a decade.

As Western Region Chief of Staff, she serves as the strategic advisor and thought partner to the Syntaktes, managing the strategic operations and governance of a regional structure serving over 1,100 members across multiple states and countries.

In July 2022, Dr. Jaye co-founded the Rhoyal Legacy Foundation, an organization dedicated to providing college scholarships to young women. She also spearheaded the establishment and chartering of the National Pan-Hellenic Council – Southern Arizona and was elected its Inaugural President.
